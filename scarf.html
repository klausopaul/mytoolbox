Communication filters - S.C.A.R.F.

A good acronym to remember some things that can help when managing, communicating or collaborating with people.

SStatus
CCertainty
AAutonomy
RRelatedness
FFairness

SCARF represents the different types of "filters" that happen when a message is sent across from one person to another (or to a group of persons).
